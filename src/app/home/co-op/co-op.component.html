About Interfriends Co-operative

  • Interfriends Co-operative is registered in England as a registered society under the Co-operative and Community Benefit Societies Act 2014
  • As a Co-operative, we exist for the benefit of the members who have invested in our co-operative business
  • Interfriends Co-operative membership is open to interfriends members only
  • Our vision is to collectively pull our resources together to own and run businesses for the benefit of our members and the community
  • At Interfriends Co-operative, we believe in the power of leveraging our efforts to succeed together

How our Co-operative works

  • Existing Interfriends members can apply for membership in the Co-operative
  • Purchase and own at least one share in the Interfriends co-operative
  • Your investment in the Co-operative business makes you a shareholder in the business
  • Your share in our Co-operative business entitles you to receive a dividend as the business performs and becomes profitable
  • You can withdraw your shares at any point in time by providing us with the minimum notice period
  • As a shareholder in our Co-operative business, you will have a say in how some of our services are run

How to become a member of the Co-operative

  • You have to be an existing Interfriends savings club member
  • You have to meet the minimum joining criteria to become a member
  • You should purchase a minimum of one share in the Co-operative
