Frequently Asked Questions

Interfriends is a savings club that helps members to diligently save money and also manage their finances by taking part in a savings circle.

Interfriends is also a Co-operative where members become shareholders in the Interfriends Co-operative business

Interfriends has been in operation for 15 years. We are trusted by our members for professionalism, openness, and transparency

We are a Co-operative, which means we also do many things together, and our members share in the values of the Co-operative

Interfriends membership is by recommendation only, which means members only recommend friends and family they can trust.

By joining a savings circle, you willingly hold yourself to account by agreeing to diligently save a specific amount each month with other circle members.

By joining Interfriends savings circle, you cultivate the most important habit of diligent saving, even when you don’t feel like it.

By joining Interfriends, you can have the flexibility of receiving an early bulk payout without having to wait till the end of the cycle

With traditional banks, you can only withdraw what you have saved and nothing more.
