Exclusive Membership

  • Membership in any Interfriends Circle is by recommendation only. This ensures trust and reliability within our community.
  • Each Circle is limited to up to 25 closely connected members, e.g. family, friends, school colleagues, work colleagues, etc.

Organised Cycles

  • Interfriends operate in two Cycles of six months per Cycle. All members start and end the Cycle at the same time.

Bespoke Circles

  • Apply to create a circle around you.(Control who can be in your circle; max 25 members)
